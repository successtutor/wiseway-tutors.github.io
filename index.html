    • vision

    We want to be a part of an education system that provides excellence, equity and opportunity to every child and young person in the AA; we want all pupils to receive the support they need to overcome any barriers they face in achieving their full potential; we believe the Tutor Trust model could play a positive part in the school system in every area of the country and we are committed to growing our work across and beyond the Addis Ababa.

    Mission

    Wiseway Tutors is a fruitful outcome of the intense passion for effective teaching which brings about Constructive and Progressive development in children. The pandemic and its after effects have also severely affected the teaching learning process and we hope to offer a remedy to the impairment through our Academy. Our primary objective is to ingrain the skill set of effective learning by developing inquisitiveness, logic, reasoning and love for acquiring knowledge. With this goal in mind, we launched Top Tutor Academy on digital platform, so that we can have far and wide global reach and be a vital part of intellectual growth and development of students at all levels of learning.

    Passion

    Motivation: A great tutor is genuinely interested in helping students grow and learn. Their motivation goes beyond financial gain; they are dedicated to making a positive impact on a student’s academic journey. Consistency matters, so look for a tutor committed to working with your child over the long
    Adaptability: Recognizing that every student is unique, a good tutor adjusts their teaching approach based on individual needs. Flexibility is key, as they adapt their methods as the student progresses.
    Patience: Guiding students through challenges requires patience. Whether explaining concepts repeatedly or addressing difficulties, a tutor must demonstrate this quality. Strong subject expertise alone won’t suffice if patience is lacking.
    Enthusiasm: Passionate tutors inspire students. When children face struggles or disappointments in school, an enthusiastic tutor can ignite a love for learning. Their positive influence can transform a student’s perception of a subject.
